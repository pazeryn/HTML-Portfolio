The Best Movies According to Eryn

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Princess Mononoke

I enjoy anime a lot, and this is one I could watch endlessly.

Terminator 2: Judgement Day

This is hands down one of the best action movies ever made. I am cheering, shouting, crying... I feel like a kid again watching this movie.

Lord of the Rings: Two Towers

This movie has everything. Action, romance, fantasy, beautiful visuals, great acting, best characters, gorgeous music... I've loved it since the day I saw it.
